Purpose and importance of the Exhibit

This exhibit serves the purpose of spreading information about the canonical authors in the literature of the Philippines in the 20th and 21st century. As the technological era advances, this generation must be inspired by the artist that built the nation's literature sector, in addition to that because this generation primarily relies on technology, we have purposefully made this exhibit a website, for it is accessible to everyone for any age in just a click of a button.
